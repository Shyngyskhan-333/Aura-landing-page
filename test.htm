Why "Aura"?

We decode human intent to provide a truly unique mediation experience.

Deep Psychological Context
Unlike standard AI that flags individual agressive phrases, Aura analyzes the historical context, passive aggression, and manipulative framing of the entire conversation.
Proactive De-escalation
Contestants provide "stress score", while Aura acts as an active mediator, generating exact phrasing to de-escalate tension and reach a constructive outcome.
Zero-Trace Privacy
Other platforms train their models on private conflicts. Aura uses a strict Zero-Trace architecture, ensuring that sensitive corporate and personal chats remain strictly private.
